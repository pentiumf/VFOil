VIRGIN FIELDS CORPORATION

Virgin Fields LLC was formed to meet the challenges of the Oil and Gas industry by ensuring products and services are delivered efficiently to meet our Customer’s needs.

Virgin Fields Company is committed to being a premier vault of knowledge in the oil and gas business and top-tier performance by creating services through sustainable, efficient growth in delivering quality Petroleum and Petrochemical Products, LPG Gas and the right services and durable spare parts, equipment supply for the oil drilling and refinery industry, thereby contributing to the world’s energy security. Along with Petro Chemical Products, Virgin Fields also specializes in the trading Crude Oil to our valuable clientele.

Our goal is to assist the African Oil & Gas Industry by providing access to quality petroleum and petrochemical products from the international market via strategic Alliances/Partnerships and Joint Ventures. Virgin Fields LLC with its Global Partners will help to businesses and countries to meet their aspirations towards growth, profitability, development and be a player on the international stage.

Virgin Fields also assists our associate mining firm based in Indonesia for the supply of Coal across the globe. We handle our clients through the entire process including introduction to the mining company, coal mine visit and introductory/ reviewing meeting at the headquarters in Jakarta till the time the allocation reached our customer. We are quite keen to ensure that our customers are completely satisfied with the service provided and our mining associates till date boast a Zero Percent Delivery failure record since its inception in 1998.

Virgin Fields together with our Financial Partners are into the business of financing credible and profitable infrastructure projects across the globe.

ENERGY DEVELOPMENT
