OIL & GAS TRADE

Tank Farm - Caribbean

Virgin Fields together with its partners are engaged in the business of managing a Petroleum Tank Farm called Port Kaiser which is a centrally located tank storage facility on the South Coast of Jamaica, with infrastructure to efficiently service Jamaica and the regions with a supply of refined finished fuel products from its tanks and pier. It is a duty-free zone, which makes it a strategic holding facility. This has presented us with the opportunity to service the nearby islands in the Caribbean that are in desperate need of fuel including Haiti (614,306 Metric Tons fuel/year), Republica Dominicana (DR, 6,091,865 Metric Tons fuel/year), and Belize (358,345 Metric tons fuel/year). DR is the largest USER of fuel in the Caribbean, followed by Curacao, then Jamaica.
The tanks have a capacity of 562,000 barrels (84,151 Metric Tons). The tanks are serviced exclusively by a 575’ pier capable of servicing up to a 360’ tankers. The draft is 35 feet.

Tank Farm - Ghana

Virgin Fields LPG Tank Farm gas operations are the state of the art solutions in delivering gas to end users via our distribution depots/stations. Our Gas tank farm project and automated loading gantry is to be used as a source to eliminate shortage of gas and to ensure steady supply to end users. Here at Virgin Fields our expertise in the gas procurement, storage and delivery ensures efficient management of Gas deliveries under gas contracts with utilization of Gas Management System. We guarantee efficient Gas nomination, scheduling and Allocation for onward distribution.
Expanding this project further is to have in place storage facility for PMX/Gasoline of 30,000 metric tonnes capacity and AGO-Diesel of 60,000 Metric tonnes capacity.
